Restoration Practices

What does the final product look like?

Photo © Disney

When it comes to Disney DVD releases, everyone is a critic.  There is an entire rather informed community online that devotes itself to reviews of DVD releases, and the subset of that community with a special interest in Disney is no small fraction of the whole. 


Many of the issues outlined in the restoration entry will attest to some of the reasons for these discrepancies, but there is still much left to the imagination.  With all of these available versions, which is the most accurate record of the original textures, grain levels, color densities, and sound quality? 


The comparisons on this page were done by Chuck Pennington, who meticulously compares identical frames from multiple versions of Disney VHS, laserdisc and DVD releases to reveal discrepancies.  Chuck has graciously agreed to allow me to include these on my page. 

Thank you  Chuck!
