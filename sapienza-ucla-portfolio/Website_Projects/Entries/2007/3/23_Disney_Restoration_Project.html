Disney Restoration Project

 

The term project for the Archaeology of the Medium class (MIAS 220) was to trace the development of a particular filming technology and then relate it to an aspect of current archival practice.  For my project, I completed a website about Disney Animation filming technologies, and how they relate to their current restoration practices.
