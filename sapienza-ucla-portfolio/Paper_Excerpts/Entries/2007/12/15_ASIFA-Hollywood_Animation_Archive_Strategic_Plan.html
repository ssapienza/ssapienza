ASIFA-Hollywood Animation Archive Strategic Plan

 


For the Collection Development course (MIAS 240), my final project was a Strategic Plan for the ASIFA-Hollywood Animation Archive in Burbank.  The purpose of this plan was to provide an overview of the archive’s mission, purpose, history and future goals, and to contextualize it within the framework of the broader communities of archives and animation. It set priorities and outlined a feasible timeline in which to accomplish them.


The document is a real working asset to ASIFA-Hollywood, who will use it as a guideline for both internal members and outside agencies that could potentially support its implementation.


Below, I have included the Table of Contents, to give an overview of the plan’s structure.  I also included a portion of the Strengths and Weaknesses section that articulates my notion of building “archival fans.”  Lastly, I included the External Threats and Opportunities section, which showcases how I have learned to synthesize my knowledge of the role of moving image archives in the context of an evolving digital culture.
